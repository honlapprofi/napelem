Plugin to view the last visit of googlebot to your pages, entries and woocommerce products.

== Description ==

Simple Googlebot Visit is a plugin that lets you know at all times the last time that the googlebot visited any of your content. This is important because it is in charge of indexing all your pages and showing them in the search results of Google.

Thanks to Simple Googlebot Visit you have this valuable information integrated into your Wordpress administration panel, without having to integrate tools such as Search Console.

== Installation ==

1. Install Simple Googlebot Visit either via the WordPress.org plugin repository or by uploading the files to your server.
2. Activate Simple Googlebot Visit.
3. Navigate to the Simple Googlebot Visit tab (inside setting) and define in which tables you want the Simple Googlebot Visit column to appear.
4. Navigate to the management pages where the column of Simple Googlebot Visit is activated and check the last googlebot visit.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Who should use Simple Googlebot Visit? =

Simple Googlebot Visit is perfect for business owners, bloggers, designers, developers, photographers, and basically everyone else. If you care about SEO, Simple Googlebot Visit is one more tool for your fight.

= How is the data collected? =

Simple Googlebot Visit does not consume any third party services. The data is collected on each googlebot visit. For this reason it's possible that you will not see any visit reflected until after a few hours.
